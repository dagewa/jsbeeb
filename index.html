The Waterman Beeb disc archive

Little Miss Power Hood

Click the disc to load the menu.

There are other versions of the game on the disc. Use the Break key (mapped to F12) to get back to the Acorn MOS prompt and enter "*." to list the disc contents.

Education

Click the disc to load the menu.

A collection of educational programs for primary school children.

Disc 1

Click the disc to list the contents.

There are some BASIC programs and some data files. It isn't yet clear what these are.

Janpic etc.

Click the disc to list the contents.

Various sound and graphics demos, including a synthesiser version of Golden Brown.

Best of Acorn User

Click the disc to list the contents.

The MENU does not work, and nor do all the programs here. Those that do include a Sprite Designer.

Speech!

Click the disc to load the menu.

A software speech synthesiser by Superior Software.

Biorhythms

Click the disc to load the INDEX program.

The index seems to be broken. Some of these programs work, including the title program, BIO

Let's Compute, Programs

Click the disc to list the contents.

Some game demos and other things from Let's Compute magazine. DANGER1 and CAR are good. The purpose of other files, such as WITCH1 to 13, are unknown.

Circuits

Click the disc to list the contents.

Most of these appear to be data files - perhaps custom levels for a driving game. The game DANGER works. This is also found on the Let's Compute disc.

Original Welcome

Click the disc to start from the TITLE

Running the TITLE program works through a series of demonstration programs. The instructions say to set PAGE to &0E00.

David's No.1

Click the disc to list the contents.

Some of these seem to be maths test programs, such as 7/8, LEARNER and HYPER-M (use maths to fight an enemy spaceship that looks like Elite's Krait).

Also various chasing games, possibly related to Miss Hood. For example, DOGBONE: as a dog, chase a mobile bone.

David's No.2

Click the disc to load the menu.

Unfinished versions of Little Miss Power Hood and other things. Many broken. What is the *SCALE command?

David 3

Click the disc to list the contents. There are only 3 files. It's unclear what these are.

David's Experimental Programs

Click the disc to load an intro program. Answer "Y" to the second question!

David's Experimental Programs 2

Click the disc to list the contents

CH. "PAT1" to get a series of patterns. 7/8 and 9/10 are maths tests. Some simple sound demos.

David's Experimental Programs 3

Click the disc to list the contents.

Some game cheats and example programs. GAME200 is a reaction speed test, which works well.

David Waterman

Click the disc to list the contents.

There is a sand particle simulator, SAND1, which works well. Some files, like ZALSCR, are images to load into SAND1.

David Waterman backup disc

Click the disc to list the contents.

Programs and files most of which are also found on other discs.

David's Text No. 1

Click the disc to list the contents

Files for the wordprocessor Interword, which requires a ROM that is not available in the emulator.
